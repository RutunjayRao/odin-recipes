Aloo fry

Description:

Potato fry is easy enough to make flavorful, savory and delicious dish of sauteed potatoes a.k.a aloo. This is a North Indian style recipe of potatoes sauteed with bold fragrant spices. It is also a vegan recipe made without onion and garlic. Also known as aloo fry, this dish makes for an excellent side dish with any North Indian main course meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoon oil
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • Asafoetida powder
  • 2 finely chopped chillies
  • curry leaves
  • 4 chopped potatoes
  • salt
  • turmeric
  • finely chopped coriander

Steps to make:

  1. firstly, in a large kadai heat 2 tbsp oil.
  2. add 1 tsp cumin, pinch hing, 2 chilli and few curry leaves. splutter the tempering without burning.
  3. add 4 aloo, ½ tsp salt and fry for a minute. make sure to cut the aloo on equal sizes and rinse well.
  4. now add ¼ tsp turmeric and mix well
  5. cover and cook on low flame for 10 minutes or until the aloo is cooked well.
  6. oven the kadai and roast for a minute, giving a crunchy texture.
  7. also, add 2 tbsp coriander and mix well.
